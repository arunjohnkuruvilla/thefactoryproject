Our Team

Our core is built around the beliefs and ideas of intrinsically curious, nine young entrepreneurs

A moment from our two year anniversary

Over a cup of sulaimani, on the midnight of 15th December 2014, nine room mates came up with the idea of launching a customized apparel brand. The team was united by the idea of helping customers create memories and a sense of belongingness filled with emotions attached to their schools, colleges, groups and gangs in the form of t-shirts, sweatshirts and jerseys. They also aimed at building an ultimate destination that raised their customer’s dreams of keeping memories alive
